Wirral Wanderers Football Club competes in the Chester & Wirral Football League, Birkenhead Sunday League and the PES Master League. Established in 2010, The Wanderers are 100% fan owned and are funded by the Wirral Wanderers Supporters Trust.

The club enjoys strong links with local businesses and is proud to be sponsored by not one, but two local breweries. Operating with a strictly not-for-profit business model, all profits are re-invested back into the local community through local community groups and charities.

The Wanderers play their home games at the historic "Trappers' Field" at Birkenhead Park, opened in 1847 as the first publicly funded civic park in the world. The club currently shares its pitches with the local Cricket Club, and enjoys post-match socials at the ground's famous clubhouse, built in 1849.
